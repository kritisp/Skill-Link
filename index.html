Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, contact us.

How does the Skill Credit system work?

It's simple! For every hour you spend teaching a skill to a community member, you earn one Skill Credit. You can then use that credit to 'pay' for one hour of learning from someone else. It's a cashless economy designed to value everyone's time equally.

Is SkillLink safe to use?

Safety is our top priority. We have a multi-step verification process for members, including optional background checks for tutors. All communication happens through our secure, in-app chat, and our peer-review system helps maintain a high standard of trust and quality in the community.

What if I don't think I have a skill to teach?

You'd be surprised! Skills aren't just about professional qualifications. You can teach anything from baking bread, speaking a language, basic gardening, to how to organize a closet. Our platform is designed to help you identify and share your unique talents.

How is this different from other learning platforms?

Most platforms focus on pre-recorded video courses. SkillLink is about live, one-on-one, in-person (or live video) interaction with people in your local community. We focus on building real-world connections, not just consuming content.
